Cryptic Word

1 The sultry horn as cryptic word --  
2 as caffeinated ennui, 
3 as spiritual nasal-spray,
4 as a hundred-thousand particles in mind at play -- 
5 blares freedom exists 
6 in that which cannot be held.

7 In the long note of a slow melody
8 that paints from a blue and green palette 
9 with thick, heavy brushstrokes,
10 the mind sees proof in the unseen,
11 enough to go about the day 
12 with a belief in metaphysics.

12.1.18
