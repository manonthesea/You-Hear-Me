What Could Have Been

1 Fiameta lives in fog
2 unknowing of what is.
3 Seeming eyes earth-brown 
4 and green like grass,
5 bring her alive within lines.
 
6 Fiameta is and was
7 troubled caused.
8 Forever her existence 
9 commingles with grace
10 on a thread-bound page.
 
11 Fiameta is slow to walk
12 the streets after dark.
13 Hearing baby bawling,
14 lady lying, and homeless dying, 
15 she pretends and portends.
 
16 Fiameta gives faint recognition
17 to brought-down passerby.
18 Yet even when looking into her eyes
19 as they were theirs,
20 they read an unwritten page.

21 Fiameta laughs at slightest misconception,
22 seeing life in a different light.
23 Through distant-streetlamp, dim-lit world,
24 and shadow of the real thing,
25 she speaks here and now.

26 Fiameta lives in fog
27 unknowing of everything
28 that is and ever was.
29 Fiameta is, but then again,
30 is no more than the page.

31 Circa 2003
