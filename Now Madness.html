Now Madness

1 Now madness hides in the virus,
2 but semblance was lost in the host.
3 A twitch so common, some call it locomotion.
4 A pounding so hard, it hides from where it comes.
5 The world so ajar, it hangs off its hinges.

6 It seems and seems, and the seeming is incessant. 
7 It seems Pilate is a well-liked man,
8 Judas turns a profit off his investment,
9 and the devil lives in a penthouse suite with an open door,
10 where some dare to go.

11 I put my face in my unwashed hands
12 with no regard for the novelty
13 of death and fear in our time
14 without communion.

15 Ever-seeming, if a Savior ever was, he is gone.
16 Everyone is gone, neither here nor in the now.

8.11.20
