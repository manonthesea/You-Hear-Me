Winter

1At the card table Saturday night,
2we sat as our spirits were somewhere else.
3Winter neared, and we were caught
4in the snowstorm of our conscience. 

5Emmanuel is hard to see on the horizon, 
6hidden by the ineffability of a blizzard,
7not of the sky but of the mind.
8Place and time fix as certainty spins.

9The word is the form everlasting
10of a storm that cannot be grasped.
