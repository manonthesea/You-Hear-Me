How Soon We Forget

1 Water jumps and tumbles to the wet sand
2 splashing bare legs as a single sheet of frozen glass.

3 Tepidity abounds as the sun rejuvenates
4 the world at a molecular level with the promise of an aspiring year.

5 Waves thrust upon the skin cut to the bone
6 with ecstasy that is cold water in premature spring.

7 The wind and the chill of the planet today help forget
8 how hot the sun burns in the coming-of-age century.
2.27.17
