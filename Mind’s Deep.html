Mind’s Deep

1Leave the paradigms of our time,
2and travel mind’s deep.
    
3In the ever-expanse and constantly begun, 
4a world exists where Christ lives 
5despite being hammered to a tree here in our world.

6You and I being perpetually reborn
7is a sublime pattern repeating within the cloth of reality.
8I tell you this not because it matters here and now
9but because hope appears not to the watchful eye.

10Possibilities abound but the implication of the ultimate will
11will be worked out in all the reproductions and interpretations
12seen by the closed eye while the mind sleeps.

13I tell you word is bound to bring us Love alive
14when faith is had in what can be imagined --
15that the Father will scatter the ashes of his Son
16like seeds when chance outdoes probability
17and that the same you, I, and Him exist again and again.
1.11.19
