Quite Like My Love for You

1 All the flowers in the world,
2 I’ll give to you,
3 if you will be mine.
4 All mine.  Me me me.

5 When I am yours, 
6 then you should expect
7 all the flowers in the world,
8 for I’ll be a gardener in spring.

9 My flowers they grow and grow
10 for you, my honeybee.

Circa 2005
