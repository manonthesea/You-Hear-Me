12.31.19

1 Outback, on the porch of the decade, 
2 the purposeful serenity of time
3 sculpts rock slowly in the final hours:
4 the year is countless raindrops into a stream,
5 flowing like water through bedrock,
6 pulling down flotsam and jetsam 
7 (victim to the drive toward the sea). 

8 Flooding light upon a silhouetted figure
9 reveals the wondrous shape unearthed
10 by the infinite strikes of a chisel point.
11 This night buoys hope that meaning delivers
12 into the bay of the coming future
13 by a force greater than the planet’s hold
14 upon space and time.
