Quantum Nothingness

1 It just takes the right voice,
2 and if you’re anything like me,
3 the pulse is full and beyond trembling
4 as you exit space and put your hands on time.

5 She smiles while shaking her red hair ablaze,
6 and if you’re anything like me,
7 the spirit is full and beyond trembling
8 as God enters into this room a mess.

9 Continue counting the seconds with amazement,
10 and if you’re anything like me,
11 the dog sits on your lap, and you feel her fur frizzing
12 and know grace exists in this page’s quantum nothingness.
