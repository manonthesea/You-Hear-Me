Le Maison du René Magritte

1 One light, one large leafy tree.
2 What one couple loves in window
3 lit with color of somber fire?
4 Out front sits the house walking on water,
5 upstairs aflame, fire on water.

6 Subtle with softest twilight palette,
7 waning sky of blue hue holds clouds – 
8 pure white – painted above the night. 
9 One lamp lights the very center, the heart.
10 Then it’s him and her up the stairs
11 in the burning-amber room mimicking the art.
12 Bowler-hat-wearing-lover lies 
13 in fiery passion of artist’s imagination
14 and knows not whether it day or night
15 while coloring vast canvas of his creation.
Circa 2003
