The Bard Speaks

1 In my heart, a rip,
2 a gnawing reminder of the past
3 that tugs at my spirit,
4 whispering tales of yore.

5 A silence falls,
6 heavy with unspoken words,
7 the air thick with dreams deferred,
8 of hopes shattered and mended.

9 I roam through memories,
10 tracing the paths I once walked,
11 where laughter echoed,
12 and joy danced in the sunlight.

13 Yet here I stand,
14 a bard with a fractured tune,
15 weaving words into the void,
16 searching for the harmony lost.

17 So listen close,
18 for the rip in my heart sings,
19 a melody of longing,
20 a song of the unforgotten.

21 In the echoes of silence,
22 in the spaces between breaths,
23 you’ll find the truth of my being,
24 the essence of the bard within.

25 So I’ll carry on,
26 with each step, each word,
27 stitching the rip in my heart
28 into a tapestry of life.

29 A tapestry woven with dreams,
30 hopes, and endless possibilities,
31 for in every rip, there lies
32 the chance to create anew.

33 In my heart, a rip,
34 but also a spark of light,
35 guiding me through the shadows,
36 toward the dawn of understanding.

37 And as the sun rises,
38 illuminating the path ahead,
39 I embrace the rip,
40 for it is part of my song.

41 A song that will echo,
42 in the hearts of those who listen,
43 a reminder that we are all
44 connected through our stories.

45 So here I stand,
46 a bard with a rip in my heart,
47 ready to share my song,
48 with the world once more.
