Friday Irony

1 Their young bodies unsettled in the Good Friday service 
2 cause the pew to pray for faith and strength to tolerate 
3 the deteriorating mood of those youth
4 sent to play as the men mourn the slain son.

5 No piety exists in the screams 
6 raining down on the diminutive faces
7 of boys made to mourn slain men
8 from a foreign land with an indiscernible tongue
9 as the automobile moves away
10 from the holy rites of an unnerving Friday.
3.30.18
