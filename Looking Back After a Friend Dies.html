Looking Back After a Friend Dies

1 Praises go up 
2 for the little old black dog 
3 who sunbathes in the grass
4 as heat rises visibly alive.

5 Blessings come down
6 because our world aflame is loved
7 as that dog loves me 
8 steady through fire.

9 Hands raise
10 in a benediction unknowing
11 yet prescient as time existing 
12 on the other side of yesterday.

7.20.18
