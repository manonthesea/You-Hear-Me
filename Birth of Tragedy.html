Birth of Tragedy

1Bottled spirit of sacred séance, 
2born in soil, bathed in decay,
3none more beautiful in such a way,
4as when nightly relish ferments 
5into Dionysian intent.

6Lovers lie at ends yet inseparable,
7replaying games of nightly ritual
8made habit moons ago,
9as still dawning room mends
10from midnight revelry bends.

11Stale mouths kiss again,
12where dry, stilled spirit hangs,
13drowning away pains,
14as midday shots of irreverent bliss
15down until dark and into drunkenness.
Circa 2003
