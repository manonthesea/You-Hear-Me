Love in Time

1.

1 Love me tender. Love me sweet.
2 Love me when grave claims my feet.
3 When day, when night have gone,
4 past morning, before first bird’s song,
5 love me always, as long as we exist as one with God.

2.

6 Oh God, uncertainty wants to cry aloud.
7 Today, tomorrow, and the next — forever looms,
8 and life seems always a closing in, dark room,
9 until my lips feel warm breath
10 undoing my doubt of unfathomable depth.
9.4.10
